Purpose

Welcome to my online math notes! This site aims to provide a comprehensive set of free online and downloadable notes on introductory real analysis. I've written these notes assuming no prior knowledge, aside from the standard prerequisite material required for the relevant course.

Currently, I have notes available on the construction of the real numbers, including their axioms and properties. Over time, I plan to expand the content to include both introductory and rigorous calculus, linear algebra, and potentially topics within complex analysis.

I've made lessons on this site available for download in PDF format. To access the downloadable version of any lesson, simply navigate to that topic and select the download button.

Lesson Layout

The content on the website is organised into topics, sections, and lessons. "Topics" refer to the broad subjects explored, "sections" cover the different areas within those topics, and "lessons" are the individual lessons within each section.
