Welcome Message

Welcome to my online maths notes! This website is aimed at students seeking extra help with introductory real analysis. I am entirely self-taught and working on this project as a hobby, so there will be mistakes in some of the content. However, I have done my best to ensure everything is as accurate as possible. If you spot a mistake or want to provide feedback, you can contact me at archimedes314notes@gmail.com.

Purpose and Scope

This website focuses on using mathematical logic to construct the real numbers and to understand their properties. We first learn about the basics of logic: what the symbols mean, and how we use them to prove mathematical concepts. From our basic logic, we can construct a simple axiomatic system to define the integers and rational numbers in terms of natural numbers, along with operations such as addition and multiplication. After we have a basic understanding, we return to logic to develop a deeper understanding, thereby building a stronger system to define the irrational numbers and strengthening our definitions of the naturals, integers, and rationals. In the future, this website will be expanded to include lessons on linear algebra and calculus.

All lessons on this website come with a downloadable PDF. Additionally, the source code is publicly available on GitHub and is licensed under the MIT licence.
